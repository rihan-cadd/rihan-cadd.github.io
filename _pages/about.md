Hello! I am a **Ph.D. Candidate** in the Division of Chem/Bioinformatics at **Chung-Ang University**, advised by Prof. [Yoonji Lee](https://sites.google.com/view/cau-chembioinfo/home).

My research focuses on **Computer-Aided Drug Design (CADD)**, specifically bridging the gap between physics-based simulations and data-driven AI. I am passionate about uncovering the selectivity mechanisms of **GPCRs (Purinergic receptors)** and developing **Generative AI pipelines** for precise lead optimization.

Currently, I am working on building a hybrid framework that integrates **Free Energy Perturbation (FEP)** with **Deep Generative Models** to accelerate structure-based drug design.

### 🔬 Research Interests
I am interested in applying computational methods to solve biological problems, with a focus on:
* **Target:** G Protein-Coupled Receptors (GPCRs), Membrane Proteins
* **Methods:** Molecular Dynamics (MD), Free Energy Perturbation (FEP)
* **Docking:** Protein-Ligand Docking, Virtual Screening
* **AI/ML:** Generative AI for SBDD, Graph Neural Networks (GNN)
