## 🔬 Research Interests

My research focuses on **Computer-Aided Drug Design (CADD)**, bridging the gap between physics-based simulations and data-driven AI.
* **Core Topics:** Virtual Screening, Structure-Based Drug Design (SBDD), GPCR Selectivity (P1/P2Y)
* **Methodologies:** Free Energy Perturbation (FEP), Molecular Dynamics (MD), Graph Neural Networks (GNN), Generative AI
